Format Guide

Document System · Quick reference

One template family,
three levels of importance.

Every Korea Dream Path document shares the same header, footer, and four-point star. Edit any field directly on the page, then save as PDF or Word — ready for Google Docs, Word, or Hancom.

Importance — 3 levels

Standard · Deep Indigo #1E1654Routine, day-to-day documents
Important · Vivid Purple #6B2DBEConfidential or decision-bearing
Priority · Star Gold #F4B72EAction required, time-critical

Brand palette — 3 tones only

Star Gold#F4B72E
Vivid Purple#6B2DBE
Deep Indigo#1E1654

To keep the star meaningful, it appears only in these three tones. No other colour is used for it.

Page anatomy

1Header — logo lockup + EDUCATION INITIATIVE descriptor, document type, and the importance star chip.
2Accent bar — a top rule in the importance colour.
3Footer — sender name, full postal address, and koreadreampath.com.
4Page number — centred at the foot of every page. For multi-page documents use Page X of Y (as on this guide).

Don'ts

×Don't recolour the star — only the three brand tones.
×Don't invent extra levels — there are exactly three.
×Don't stretch, rotate, or recolour the logo.
×Don't mix two importance colours in one document.
×Don't set body text on the Deep Indigo bar.
×Don't use emoji — use the star and line icons.

Typeface, sizes & downloads.

Typeface — Pretendard

Aa 가Light 300 Aa 가Regular 400 Aa 가Medium 500 Aa 가Bold 700

One family for Latin and Hangul. Headings in Deep Indigo at 700; body at 400; eyebrow labels in uppercase with wide tracking. Links and accents in Vivid Purple.

Download Pretendard 1.3.9 (.zip)

Source & web fonts: github.com/orioncactus/pretendard · In Word: install the .ttf files, then set the font to Pretendard.
